ThinkSafe RCA Pro - Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 28, 2025

ThinkSafe Strategies LLC ("we," "our," or "us") operates the ThinkSafe RCA Pro mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email Address: Required to create an account and sign in
  • Password: Securely stored and encrypted by Firebase Authentication

Root Cause Analysis (RCA) Data

When you create RCA reports, you may enter:

  • Incident details and descriptions
  • Job numbers and customer information
  • Location information (manually entered, not GPS)
  • Contributing factors and corrective actions
  • 5 Whys analysis data

Usage Data

  • App usage analytics (via Firebase Analytics)
  • Crash reports (via Firebase Crashlytics)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide Services: To create, store, and manage your RCA reports
  • AI Features: To generate suggested contributing factors, 5 Whys analysis, and root cause summaries (Pro feature)
  • Improve the App: To fix bugs and improve user experience
  • Customer Support: To respond to your inquiries

3. AI Processing

When you use our AI-assisted features (Pro subscription), your incident narrative and related data are sent to OpenAI's API for processing. OpenAI processes this data according to their privacy policy. We do not use your data to train AI models.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored securely in Google Firebase (Firestore)
  • All data transmission is encrypted using TLS/SSL
  • Your RCA data is private and only accessible to your account
  • We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service Providers: Firebase (Google) for authentication and data storage, OpenAI for AI features
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export: Export your RCAs as PDF documents
  • Correct: Update or correct your information

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

8. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the App and updating the "Effective Date."

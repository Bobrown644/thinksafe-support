Frequently Asked Questions

What is ThinkSafe RCA Pro?

ThinkSafe RCA Pro is a Root Cause Analysis app designed for electrical utility contractors. It helps you document incidents, perform 5 Whys analysis, identify contributing factors, and generate professional PDF reports.

What's included in the free version?

The free version includes:

  • Create and manage unlimited RCA reports
  • Manual 5 Whys analysis
  • Manual contributing factors entry
  • PDF export
  • Cloud sync across devices

What does Pro unlock?

ThinkSafe RCA Pro ($14.99 one-time purchase) adds AI-powered features:

  • AI-generated Contributing Factors
  • AI-generated 5 Whys Analysis
  • AI-generated Root Cause Summary

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings in the app and tap "Restore Purchases". Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID you used to purchase.

Is my data secure?

Yes. Your data is stored securely in Google Firebase with encryption. Only you can access your RCA reports. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Can I use the app offline?

The app requires an internet connection to sync data and use AI features. PDF export works offline once the RCA is loaded.

How do I delete my account?

Contact us at support@thinksafestrategies.com and we will delete your account and all associated data within 30 days.
